About Us

Welcome to Loisoi Ranch and Resort, where luxury meets nature in the heart of Kenya’s picturesque Nyeri region. Whether you’re seeking a serene escape, an adventurous holiday, or a place to host your next special event, Loisoi offers everything you need for an unforgettable experience. Our resort is designed to provide modern comforts while immersing you in the beauty and tranquility of Kenya’s landscapes.

We believe luxury should be accessible to everyone. Our services are designed to provide a high-end experience at a price that fits your budget.

Why Choose Loisoi Ranch and Resort?

Experience the perfect balance of comfort and adventure. From modern rooms to luxurious safari tents, Loisoi combines high-end amenities with breathtaking natural surroundings


Sustainability


We’re committed to eco-friendly practices, from using sustainable materials to supporting local communities, ensuring that we leave a positive impact on both the environment and the people of Kenya.

Why Choose Loisoi Ranch and Resort?

Whether you choose our Kifaru Cottage, inspired by Kenya’s traditional architecture, or opt for the spacious Africana Cottage for a group retreat, our accommodations provide a unique blend of luxury and culture.
